Live The Experience of A Gateway in Banyuwangi

With its unique position at the eastern tip of the Java Island, Banyuwangi hosts some of Indonesia’s most extraordinary sights, offering adventures and fulfilling travel experiences. Mount ljen’s phenomenal blue fire crater and beautiful natural parks; challenging surf waves and remarkable underwater life in renown beaches; local festivities, art and culture are just several fascinating tourist attractions in Banyuwangi.

Located on Jalan Raya Jember, just 20 minutes from Banyuwangi International Airport and 5 minutes to the city center, Kokoon Hotel Banyuwangi provides 163 rooms and suites, a ballroom that accommodates up to 700 people, 8 meeting rooms for up to 50 people each room, Ijen Restaurant, Rooftop Lounge, Indoor Swimming Pool, Spa and Gym.
